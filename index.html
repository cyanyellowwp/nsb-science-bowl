    NSB Format (per RULES.md)

    • Toss-up (4 pts): Either team buzzes. In this setup, the moderator waits for the external buzz app and then presses A or L. No team consult on toss-ups (verbal/audible only — NSB allows non-verbal). Wrong answer → opposing team gets a free shot.
    • Bonus (10 pts): Only the toss-up winner plays. 20 seconds. Team consult allowed. Captain answers (per Rule 3-9 — not strictly enforced in this app).
    • Interrupt penalty: if a team buzzes before the toss-up is fully read and misses, the toss-up is re-read in full for the opposing team, who then answer it for the 4 points (awarded only if they answer correctly).
